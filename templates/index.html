About Helmet Detection System

This application uses machine learning to detect whether people are wearing helmets in images and live video.

The system uses YOLO (You Only Look Once) object detection to identify two classes:

  • Without Helmet - People not wearing proper head protection
  • With Helmet - People wearing appropriate helmets

This tool can be used for workplace safety monitoring, construction site management, and safety compliance.
